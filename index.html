Student Does Christmas

Cheap, easy student recipes for December

About

Students. Its not easy cooking christmas food most of the time. So we've given you tonnes of easy, delicious Christmas recipes to use.



Starters

French Onion Soup

1. Chop up 5 white onions and 5 red onions very finely 2. Fry gently in lots of butter until soft (c.20mins) 3. Stir in 1 tbsp cornflour stirring to avoid lumps 4. Add 1.5litres of stock, bring to boil, and simmer for 20mins 5. Serve topped with cheesy croutons

Mains

Baked Camembert

1. Heat the oven to 200' 2. Chop up 2 garlic cloves very finely and stick them evenly across the cheese 3. Add 2 tablespoons of wine to the cheese 4. Wrap it in foil and bake for 30mins until warm and gooey 5. Serve with boiled potatoes and bread

Desserts

Chestnut Meringues

1. Heat the oven to 120' 2. Whip 4 egg whites until stiff 3. Fold 200g caster sugar gradually 4. Put heaped tablespoons onto a lined baking tray and bake for 1.5 hours 5. Whip up 200ml double cream until stiff, then add 8 roughly chopped chestnuts 6. Sandwich the meringues with the chestnut cream
